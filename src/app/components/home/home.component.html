Getting started

To get started all you have to do is create an account, free! Click on the "Events" button, "Create Event", add your details and you're done! It's that easy!


Info

Welcome to CompanyEvent! CompanyEvent is a web app for companies to post what is happening around the office. Whether than means a mandatory business meeting or an optional pitch in, companies can keep their employees up to date with what is going on. In CompanyEvent you can create new events with a Title, an Overview of the event, the Location, and lastly the Date and Time. If you make a mistake when making an event or the event gets canceled that isn't a problem in CompanyEvent. Events can easily be edited and deleted thanks to CompanyEvent's CRUD factor! So get started and keep your employees up to date!
